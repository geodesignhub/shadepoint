Smartly manage your tree canopy

Shadepoint helps cities and organizations make smarter decisions about tree planting and canopy management. By analyzing sun exposure, pedestrian movement, and existing green cover, the tool identifies optimal locations for new trees to maximize shade and comfort. Use Shadepoint to increase your urban canopy, prioritize areas with the greatest need, and create cooler, healthier public spaces.
